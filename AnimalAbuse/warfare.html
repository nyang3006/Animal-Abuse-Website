Warfare

Animals have been commonly used in warfare and it is one of the forms of cruelty because the animals end up getting killed in the end. Some examples of animals that have been used in warfare are horses, dogs, dolphins, birds, pigs, oxen, camels, rats and elephants. These animals have been used in dangerous situations in war. These situations include transport, bomb detection and also directly involved with harming the enemy. The military train these animal to do these things. As a result thousands of animals are shot, stabbed, mutilated, and killed in military actions.



Animals are not meant for fighting, they are meant to be loved.

Dogs In WW2

In war, it is difficult and risky to destroy a tank. it's why some people decided to use dogs with bomb vests. They train dogs to run towards a tank and then explode. Tank pilots are too shocked to do anything and get killed, others are worried about the dog. Some don't think it's ethical to shoot the dog and get killed. It creates a problem that no one wants to be in. This was practiced by the Soviet Union. They found it to be useful in destroying tanks however, they stopped due to the dogs running towards friendly tanks and exploding them.
